1. Privacy Policy

Sun State Digital ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website or services.

We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, we also comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Complete a contact form or enquiry form on our website
  • Book a call or appointment through our calendar system
  • Engage our services and complete onboarding
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or messaging platforms
  • Subscribe to any email communications

Personal Information We May Collect

  • Full name and business name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Business address and website URL
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card details)
  • Communications history and project notes

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time on site via cookies and analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide the digital marketing services you have engaged us for
  • Communication: To respond to enquiries, provide updates, and maintain client relationships
  • Billing: To process payments and maintain financial records as required by Australian law
  • Marketing: To send relevant information about our services — you can opt out at any time
  • Improvement: To understand how our website and services are used and improve them accordingly
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your personal information is stored securely in Australian and/or internationally recognised cloud services. We take reasonable steps to protect your information against misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with our legal obligations. Client data is retained for a minimum of 7 years for tax and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business. These may process your data on our behalf:

  • GoHighLevel (GHL): CRM and automation platform
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (anonymous usage data)
  • Google Workspace: Email and document management
  • Cloudflare / hosting providers: Website hosting and CDN

6. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act and where applicable the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sunstatedigital.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and help us understand how our site is used. We use essential cookies (required for functionality), analytics cookies (Google Analytics), and marketing cookies (Google Ads, Facebook Pixel).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies.
