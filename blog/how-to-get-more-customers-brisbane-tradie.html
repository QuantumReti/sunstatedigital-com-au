How to Get More Customers for Your Brisbane Tradie Business in 2026

The work is there. Brisbane is growing fast. But 9 in 10 people Google before they call a tradie — and if your online presence isn't pulling its weight, you're handing jobs to your competitors.

If you're a tradie in Brisbane — a plumber in Ipswich, an electrician in Chermside, or a landscaper in the Redlands — you already know the work is there. The city is growing fast. But so is your competition, and most of them have figured out something crucial: the majority of your future customers are finding their next tradie on Google, not through word of mouth.

The good news? You don't need a massive budget to compete online. You just need to show up in the right places at the right time. Here's exactly how to do it.

1. Claim and Optimise Your Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is the single most powerful free marketing tool available to any Brisbane tradie. When someone searches "plumber near me" or "emergency electrician Brisbane Northside," Google shows a map pack at the top of the results — those three businesses that appear there get the lion's share of clicks.

To get into that map pack, you need a fully optimised GBP. That means:

  • Complete every section — business name, category, address, phone number, website, hours
  • Add high-quality photos — real photos of your work, your van, your team. Listings with photos get 42% more direction requests
  • Choose the right primary category — "Plumber" not "Plumbing Services." Specificity matters
  • List all your service areas — if you cover Brisbane, Logan, and Redlands, say so explicitly
  • Post updates weekly — Google treats active profiles as more trustworthy

A landscaper in Redcliffe who took 30 minutes to properly fill out their GBP went from invisible to appearing in the top 3 for "landscaper Redcliffe" within six weeks. That's the power of this free tool — most tradies ignore it entirely.

2. Google Reviews Are Your Digital Word of Mouth

In the tradie world, reputation is everything. Google reviews are the online version of a personal recommendation — and they're even more powerful, because they're visible to every potential customer in Brisbane who searches for you.

Here's the harsh truth: a plumbing business with 4 reviews is virtually invisible compared to a competitor with 47 reviews at 4.8 stars. When someone needs an emergency hot water replacement at 7pm, they're calling whoever looks most trusted — and that's the business with the reviews.

How to Get More Google Reviews

  • Ask every single happy customer — right after you finish the job, while they're still impressed
  • Send a follow-up text with a direct Google review link (set this up once, use it forever)
  • Make it dead easy — the fewer clicks, the more reviews you'll get
  • Respond to every review, good and bad — it shows you care and improves your ranking

With our AI automation tools, this follow-up process can be completely automated — a text goes out automatically after every job, with a direct link and a friendly personalised message. Our clients typically double their review count within the first 60 days.

3. Your Website Needs to Work on Mobile — Full Stop

Here's a stat that should alarm every Brisbane tradie: over 70% of local service searches happen on a smartphone. When someone's hot water breaks on a Sunday morning, they're not sitting at a desktop computer — they're searching on their phone.

If your website isn't mobile-friendly, those potential customers are hitting the back button and calling your competitor. A slow, clunky website that's hard to navigate on a phone loses jobs. Period.

What does a good tradie website actually need?

  • A large, tappable phone number at the very top
  • A clear statement of what you do and where you do it
  • Fast loading time — under 3 seconds or people leave
  • Real photos of your work (not stock photos)
  • Your Google reviews embedded prominently
  • A simple contact form that works on mobile

At Sun State Digital, we build tradie websites that are mobile-first by design, load in under 2 seconds, and are structured to rank on Google from day one. And we do it from $199 — because we believe every Brisbane tradie deserves a professional online presence without a $5,000 price tag.

4. Appear in "Near Me" Searches

The phrase "near me" has exploded in search volume. "Electrician near me," "plumber near me," "landscaper near me" — these are your highest-value search terms, because the person searching is ready to hire right now.

To appear in these searches, you need three things working together:

  1. An optimised Google Business Profile with accurate location data
  2. A website that mentions your service areas — including specific Brisbane suburbs
  3. Consistent NAP data — your Name, Address, and Phone number must be identical everywhere online (website, GBP, Facebook, trade directories)

An electrician we worked with in Logan was losing jobs to a competitor based 20km away — simply because their competitor had better local SEO signals. After sorting their GBP and website, they were appearing in "near me" results within their actual service area within weeks.

5. Why Tradies Lose Jobs to Competitors Online

The most common reason Brisbane tradies lose online leads isn't price — it's response time. Research consistently shows that 90% of jobs go to the first business to respond. If someone fills out a quote form on your website at 6pm and you see it the next morning, they've already booked someone else.

The second biggest reason is trust signals. A website with no reviews, no photos, and no clear service area looks like a risk. Customers choose the option that feels safest — and that usually means the business with the best-looking online presence.

The tradies winning in Brisbane right now aren't necessarily the best at their trade — they're the ones who've made it easiest to find them, trust them, and contact them.

The Bottom Line

Getting more customers for your Brisbane tradie business in 2026 comes down to four things: showing up on Google, looking trustworthy, being easy to contact, and responding fast. None of this requires a huge budget — it requires the right setup.
